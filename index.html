Biography

I am a 1st year Ph.D. student at MMLab of The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Dahua Lin. Prior to this, I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2024.

My current research focuses on:
◆ Multi-modality
◆ Large Vision-Language Models: VQA, Video Understanding, Multi-modal Retrieval, ...
